Health Tips

Nutrition Advice

German Shepherds benefit from high-quality protein sources. Include lean meats, fish, and eggs in their diet for optimal muscle health. Avoid grains if your dog has allergies.

Nutrition

Exercise Recommendations

Adult German Shepherds need 1-2 hours of exercise daily. Combine walks with mental stimulation activities to keep them healthy and happy. Avoid strenuous exercise in puppies.

Exercise

Hip Dysplasia Prevention

Maintain a healthy weight to reduce stress on joints. Consider joint supplements with glucosamine and chondroitin for added support. Provide orthopedic bedding.

Joint Health

Dental Care

Brush your dog's teeth 2-3 times a week to prevent periodontal disease. Dental chews can help reduce plaque buildup between brushings. Regular vet dental checkups are essential.

Dental Health
